Punjabi culture is a vibrant, ancient, and resilient blend of traditions from the Punjab region (India/Pakistan), characterized by high-energy dance (Bhangra/Giddha), soul-stirring music, rich, hearty cuisine, and deep hospitality. Rooted in agriculture, it celebrates life through festivals like Lohri and Baisakhi. Key elements include the Punjabi language,, colorful attire (salwar-kameez/turban), and a strong community spirit.
